About

This map shows buildings from OpenStreetMap that are located within Bulgarian nature reserves. Buildings are categorised based on their OSM tourism tag value. Zoom in to see the reserves and building footprints.

Crafted by @simeonnedkov. Code hosted on github, map made with CartoDB. Maki icons by MapBox. Hat tip to @antitoxic and @yurukov for inspiration and data.
